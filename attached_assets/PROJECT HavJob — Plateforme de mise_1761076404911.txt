### OBJECTIVE
Develop the **web platform HavJob** according to the attached documentation and design references.  
The platform connects freelancers, individuals, and companies who post or respond to missions.  

Users can:
- Post missions or offers.
- Search missions using filters (category, location, budget, etc.).
- Contact via WhatsApp or phone.
- Boost their mission or profile using payment links (Chariow checkout URLs).
